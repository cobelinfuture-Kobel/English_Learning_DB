# Theme Catalog (THEME_DB_S0)

This document catalogs all communicative themes extracted from the syllabus source (`docs/A1_C1_情境.txt`). The catalog includes standard levels (with explicit themes) and plus levels (which serve as descriptive pedagogical bridges).

---

## 1. A1 Level Themes

*   **A1-T1: 個人資訊與社交問候 (Personal Information & Greetings)**
    *   *Parent Theme:* Social Interaction
    *   *Description:* Introductions, greetings, describing basic physical features and relationships of family and friends.
*   **A1-T2: 日常生活與作息 (Daily Life & Routines)**
    *   *Parent Theme:* Daily Life
    *   *Description:* Telling time, describing daily routines, meals, and scheduling.
*   **A1-T3: 學校與教室情境 (School & Classroom)**
    *   *Parent Theme:* Education
    *   *Description:* Classroom instructions, naming school supplies, colors, and basic numbers.
*   **A1-T4: 居家與生活環境 (Homes & Neighborhoods)**
    *   *Parent Theme:* Daily Life
    *   *Description:* Describing hometowns, housing configurations, and simple directions.
*   **A1-T5: 購物與基礎交易 (Shopping & Basic Transactions)**
    *   *Parent Theme:* Transactions
    *   *Description:* Basic retail interactions, asking for prices, and buying transport tickets.
*   **A1-T6: 飲食與餐廳點餐 (Food & Dining)**
    *   *Parent Theme:* Transactions
    *   *Description:* Ordering food/takeout, expressing likes/dislikes of food and drink.
*   **A1-T7: 興趣、休閒與能力 (Interests & Abilities)**
    *   *Parent Theme:* Personal Life
    *   *Description:* Sports, hobbies, and expressing talents/abilities using "can" and "cannot".
*   **A1-T8: 旅遊、交通與天氣 (Travel & Weather)**
    *   *Parent Theme:* Travel
    *   *Description:* Simple travel plans, modes of transport, and describing weather conditions.
*   **A1-T9: 健康與醫療 (Health & Medical)**
    *   *Parent Theme:* Personal Life
    *   *Description:* Naming body parts and describing basic symptoms (e.g., headache).

---

## 2. A1+ Level (Descriptive Buffer)

*   **A1_plus-T1: A1 生活情境螺旋擴充 (Spiral Expansion of A1 Contexts)**
    *   *Parent Theme:* Daily Life (Bridge)
    *   *Description:* Descriptive only. Inherits all 9 A1 themes, but extends them by introducing past narrative structures ("what happened") and basic cause/effect logic (e.g. missing a bus because of rain). Includes letter phonics.

---

## 3. A2 Level Themes

*   **A2-T1: 日常實務與當地環境 (Daily Transactions & Local Environment)**
    *   *Parent Theme:* Daily Life
    *   *Description:* Personal/family background, geography, and basic employment/work contexts.
*   **A2-T2: 出行與消費 (Travel & Consumption)**
    *   *Parent Theme:* Transactions
    *   *Description:* Transactions in post offices, shops, and banks. Asking/giving directions, purchasing tickets, and arranging accommodation.
*   **A2-T3: 社交與討論 (Socializing & Discussion)**
    *   *Parent Theme:* Social Interaction
    *   *Description:* Planning weekend activities, making invitations, apologizing, expressing thanks, and describing past habits/experiences.

---

## 4. A2+ Level (Descriptive Buffer)

*   **A2_plus-T1: 生活素養角色扮演 (Role-play & Real Life Skills)**
    *   *Parent Theme:* Social Interaction (Bridge)
    *   *Description:* Descriptive only. Transitioning from simple sentences to paragraph-level outputs. Focuses on role-play simulation and active grammar production (like passives and perfect tenses).

---

## 5. B1 Level Themes

*   **B1-T1: 旅遊與海外生活 (Travel & Living Abroad)**
    *   *Parent Theme:* Travel
    *   *Description:* Dealing with travel emergencies, arranging accommodation, and handling complaints or refunds with authorities.
*   **B1-T2: 職場與商業環境 (Work & Business Environment)**
    *   *Parent Theme:* Work
    *   *Description:* Basic commercial communication, brainstorming in meetings, handling customer service, and writing simple reports/letters.
*   **B1-T3: 個人表達與社交 (Personal Expression & Socializing)**
    *   *Parent Theme:* Personal Life
    *   *Description:* Describing dreams, plans, and cultural themes (e.g. music, movies), and arguing abstract opinions.

---

## 6. B1+ Level (Descriptive Buffer)

*   **B1_plus-T1: 觀點論述與思辨 (Critical Discussion & Viewpoints)**
    *   *Parent Theme:* Critical Thinking (Bridge)
    *   *Description:* Descriptive only. Shift from conversational English to viewpoint debate (including AI chatting) and reading texts with polysemic depth.

---

## 7. B2 Level Themes

*   **B2-T1: 專業與學術情境 (Professional & Academic Situations)**
    *   *Parent Theme:* Academic Life
    *   *Description:* Technical discussions within one's field, reading contemporary literature, and understanding documentaries.
*   **B2-T2: 深入辯論與會議 (In-depth Debates & Meetings)**
    *   *Parent Theme:* Social Interaction
    *   *Description:* Participating in formal debates, analyzing pros/cons of policy, and defending positions in workplace meetings.
*   **B2-T3: 母語人士交流 (Native-speed Communication)**
    *   *Parent Theme:* Social Interaction
    *   *Description:* Smooth, natural interaction with native speakers at normal speed in noisy environments.

---

## 8. B2+ Level (Descriptive Buffer)

*   **B2_plus-T1: 學術與專業過渡橋樑 (Academic Bridge to C1)**
    *   *Parent Theme:* Academic Life (Bridge)
    *   *Description:* Descriptive only. Academic vocabulary expansion (4,000+ words), structured writing, and formal public speaking prep.

---

## 9. C1 Level Themes

*   **C1-T1: 高難度職場與社交 (Advanced Work & Socializing)**
    *   *Parent Theme:* Work
    *   *Description:* Leading board meetings, negotiation, handling hostile questions, and style/register switching.
*   **C1-T2: 言外之意與複雜文本 (Implicit Meanings & Complex Texts)**
    *   *Parent Theme:* Critical Thinking
    *   *Description:* Understanding sarcasm, humor, and implicit criticism in long texts and movies.
*   **C1-T3: 精準表達 (Precise Expression)**
    *   *Parent Theme:* Personal Life
    *   *Description:* Fluent, unhesitating expression on abstract topics, showing precise control of cohesive devices.
